Xu_Kai

Xu Kai (simplified Chinese: 许凯; traditional Chinese: 許凱; pinyin: Xu_Kai, born 5 March 1995), also known by his English name Kai Xu, is a Chinese actor and model. He is best known for his role as Fuheng in Story of Yanxi Palace (2018), Li Chenlan in The Legends (2019), and Gu Yanzhen in Arsenal Military Academy (2019). Added to that is his role in another hit drama Falling Into Your Smile as Lu Si Cheng and Bai Jue in Ancient Love Poetry.[1]
